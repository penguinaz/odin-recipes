Frozen Greek Yogurt Banana Slices

Description

An easy to make snack, that's convenient to store in the freezer. A good substitute for unhealthy sweet treats.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 tbsp cacao/cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Cut one banana up into even pieces.
  2. Combine greek yogurt and cacao in a bowl.
  3. Dip bananas into the chocolate yogurt to cover evenly.
  4. Place the bananas on wax paper and freeze for 2 hours.
