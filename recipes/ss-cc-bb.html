Single Serving Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Description

The beauty of this soft, fluffy chocolate chip banana bread is its simplicity in preparation. Just in a few minutes, a wonderful serving of deliciousness is ready to consume.

Ingedients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mash the banana with a fork. Whisk in the olive oil, maple syrup, egg and vanilla. Stir in the flour, baking soda and salt until well combined, then stir in the chocolate chips.
  2. Transfer to a microwave safe container. Microwave for ~2 minutes. Note: if the bottom still looks wet, microwave for extra 10 seconds.
